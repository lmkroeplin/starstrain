About STARS

Relevant, Integrated Response Readiness

Safety Training and Response Strategies, LLC has provided services to Class 1 and 2 railroads, pipelines, industrial facilities, and local units of government since 2017. Our objective is clear: provide customized, relevant training that makes your people safer and your organization more resilient.

We don't do cookie-cutter. Every training program, exercise, and contingency plan is designed around your specific operations, regulatory requirements, and risk profile. Our team brings decades of combined experience from the field — not just the classroom.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What industries does STARS serve?
STARS provides safety training and response planning for Class 1 and 2 railroads, pipelines, industrial facilities, and local, state, and federal government agencies throughout the Midwest.
What types of training does STARS offer?
OSHA compliance training (HAZWOPER, HAZCOM), HSEEP exercise design (tabletop to full-scale), contingency plan creation and review, CPR/First Aid/AED certification through the American Heart Association, fire extinguisher training, and water/river response training.
Where is STARS located?
STARS is headquartered in Bloomington, Minnesota. We operate extensively throughout the Midwest and can deploy to your location.
Can STARS design a full-scale emergency exercise?
Absolutely. We've designed and facilitated exercises ranging from tabletop discussions to full-scale operations with 150+ participants from 25+ organizations, including locomotive extrication, water operations, mock EOC activations, and live emergency alerts — all to HSEEP protocols.
Is training customized to our organization?
Every STARS program is custom-designed based on your organization's needs, regulatory requirements, and operational environment. We don't do off-the-shelf training — every lesson is built for your reality.
